## рџ§  How the Model Works

The app trains a **Random Forest Classifier** at startup (cached so it only runs once):

1. **Synthetic Data Generation** вЂ” 5,000 samples with randomized income (в‚№20KвЂ“в‚№2L), expense ratios, debt, financial goals, and risk preferences.
2. **Feature Engineering** вЂ” Derives `savings_rate`, `expense_ratio`, and `debt_to_income` ratio.
3. **Label Assignment** вЂ” Risk labels (0 = Low, 1 = Medium, 2 = High) are assigned based on rule-based thresholds, then adjusted by the user's stated risk preference.
4. **Training** вЂ” A 100-tree Random Forest with max depth 10 is fit on scaled features.
5. **Prediction** вЂ” Your inputs are preprocessed identically and passed through the trained model to produce a risk class and probability breakdown.

> **Note:** The model trains on synthetic data each session. For a production app, pre-train and serialize the model with `joblib` to avoid retraining on every cold start.
