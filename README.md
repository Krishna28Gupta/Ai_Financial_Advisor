# 🏦 AI Financial Advisor

An AI-powered personal finance dashboard built with **Streamlit** and **scikit-learn**. Enter your income, expenses, debt, and financial goals to receive a real-time risk assessment and personalized investment recommendations.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **ML-Based Risk Prediction** — A Random Forest classifier trained on 5,000 synthetic profiles predicts your financial risk level (Low / Medium / High) with confidence scores.
- **Budget Analysis** — Evaluates your spending against the 50-30-20 rule and flags over/under-budget status.
- **Investment Recommendations** — Suggests instruments (FDs, Mutual Funds, Direct Stocks) and expected return ranges based on your risk profile.
- **Savings Allocation Plan** — Breaks down monthly savings into Investment (70%), Emergency Fund (20%), and Insurance (10%).
- **Debt Health Check** — Computes your Debt-to-Income ratio and flags dangerous debt levels.
- **Live Report** — Generates a complete, formatted analysis report on every run.

## 🧠 How the Model Works

The app trains a **Random Forest Classifier** at startup (cached so it only runs once):

1. **Synthetic Data Generation** — 5,000 samples with randomized income (₹20K–₹2L), expense ratios, debt, financial goals, and risk preferences.
2. **Feature Engineering** — Derives `savings_rate`, `expense_ratio`, and `debt_to_income` ratio.
3. **Label Assignment** — Risk labels (0 = Low, 1 = Medium, 2 = High) are assigned based on rule-based thresholds, then adjusted by the user's stated risk preference.
4. **Training** — A 100-tree Random Forest with max depth 10 is fit on scaled features.
5. **Prediction** — Your inputs are preprocessed identically and passed through the trained model to produce a risk class and probability breakdown.

> **Note:** The model trains on synthetic data each session. For a production app, pre-train and serialize the model with `joblib` to avoid retraining on every cold start.

## 🖥️ Usage

1. Open the app in your browser (default: `http://localhost:8501`).
2. Fill in your details in the **left sidebar**:
   - Monthly Income (₹)
   - Monthly Expenses (₹)
   - Total Debt (₹)
   - Financial Goal (`retirement`, `house`, `education`, `emergency`)
   - Risk Preference (`low`, `medium`, `high`)
3. Click **RUN FULL AI ANALYSIS**.
4. View your complete financial report in the main panel.

## 📊 Report Sections

The generated report includes:

- **User Input Summary** — A clean recap of your inputs.
- **Risk Prediction** — Predicted risk level, confidence %, and model used.
- **Probability Breakdown** — Visual bar chart of Low / Medium / High risk probabilities.

- **Investment Recommendations** — Suggested instruments and expected annual returns.
- **Savings Allocation** — Monthly split across investment, emergency fund, and insurance.
